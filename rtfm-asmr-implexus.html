IMPLEXUS RTFM ASMR

Reading The Forgotten Manual Analog Synth Modular Review

Last update: 04/01/22

RTFM ASMR is an unauthorized video series where we Read The Forgotten Manual / Analog Synthesizer Modular Review.

It's worth your time to read the manual if one is available for the analog synthesizer or module you own. Since most people won't bother reading it for themselves, RTFM ASMR is here to read it for you.

RTFM ASMR Episode 1 is the IMPLEXUS manual.

IMPLEXUS is an analog instrument by Majella Audio, Netherlands. We own IMPLEXUS serial number 10 thanks to Early Bird backing the Kickstarter campaign. Their crowdfunding campaign successfully closed in October 2020 and IMPLEXUS arrived to us in October 2021.

Full disclosure: I volunteered to copy edit/proofread the IMPLEXUS manual for the U.S. reader. Majella Audio accepted my help pre-publication. My reward was ensuring the translations were clear and having early access to the manual. While there are many deserving manuals for RTFM ASMR series, we are happy to launch the RTFM ASMR series with IMPLEXZUS. Again, this is unauthorized and in good fun. Happy April Fool's joke day.
